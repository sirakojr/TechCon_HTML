AI, or Artificial Intelligence, is a field of computer science focused on creating systems that can perform tasks requiring human-like intelligence, such as learning, problem-solving, decision-making, and speech recognition. It works by enabling machines to perceive their environment, process data, and act to achieve goals, adapting their behavior over time by analyzing the results of their actions. AI has many usage examples, including digital assistants, personalized recommendations, and smart appliances, all of which are common parts of daily life.
